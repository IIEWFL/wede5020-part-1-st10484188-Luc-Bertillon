Working at FOUR PAWS

FOUR PAWS is the global animal welfare organisation for animals under human influence, which reveals suffering, rescues animals in need and protects them. We advocate for a world where humans treat animals with respect, empathy and understanding.


We believe in diversity within our teams and welcome everyone with respect, trust, and empathy. In our daily work, we require dedication, a global mindset, heartfelt commitment, and the desire to make a meaningful impact.

PEOPLE @ FOURPAWS

We are looking for the right people in the right places. We seek individuals who can overcome boundaries with creative solutions and strive for the impossible.

We want to attract junior talents, experts and leaders likewise. Agility, ownership and a high degree of self-responsibility is the base of collaboration within our frames.

What we look for:

  • Passionate individuals committed to animal welfare
  • Creative problem-solvers who think outside the box
  • Team players with a collaborative mindset
  • Professionals with a global perspective
  • People who value continuous learning and growth

If you share our vision and values, we would love to hear from you.

CULTURE @ FOURPAWS

We enjoy testing and learning, celebrating our successes and providing a trustful failure culture.

The strategic focus on being a vital organisation helps to optimise our organisational excellence, our effectiveness and efficiency globally.

Our Cultural Pillars:

  • Innovation: We encourage experimentation and creative thinking
  • Collaboration: We work together across teams and borders
  • Resilience: We learn from setbacks and keep moving forward
  • Transparency: We communicate openly and honestly
  • Impact: We focus on what truly makes a difference for animals

Our culture is built on mutual respect, trust, and a shared commitment to our mission.

WORKING @ FOURPAWS

We offer a digitally advanced environment with a dynamic and global spirit and state-of-the-art means of collaboration.

Working security and safety means the protection of our people, our systems and our organisation. Learn more about how we work here.

Our Work Environment:

  • Flexibility: Remote work options and flexible schedules
  • Technology: Access to the latest tools and platforms
  • Development: Continuous learning and growth opportunities
  • Wellbeing: Comprehensive health and wellness programs
  • Global Network: Collaborate with colleagues worldwide

We believe that when our team members thrive, our impact for animals grows exponentially.
