About Us

FOUR PAWS

is the global animal welfare organisation for animals under direct human influence, which reveals suffering, rescues animals in need and protects them. Our vision is a world where humans treat animals with respect, empathy and understanding.

Our Mission

At FOUR PAWS, our mission is to improve the lives of animals around the world through rescue, advocacy, and education.

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of animal lovers and professionals committed to making a difference in the lives of animals.
